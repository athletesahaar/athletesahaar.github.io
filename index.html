Athlete's Corner: Quick Tips

Pre-Workout Fuel

Consume complex carbohydrates 1-2 hours before training to ensure steady energy release throughout your session. Think oats or whole-wheat toast.

Nighttime Recovery

A small serving of slow-digesting protein (like paneer or yogurt) before bed aids muscle repair and prevents catabolism while you sleep.

Electrolyte Balance

During long sessions, you need more than water. Add a pinch of salt to your drink to replace sodium lost through sweat and prevent cramping.
